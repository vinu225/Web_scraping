Local Farmers Market Opens for Summer Season

By Sarah Johnson • June 10, 2026

The Riverside Farmers Market reopened its doors to the public on Saturday, marking the beginning of its twelfth annual summer season. Dozens of vendors set up stalls along the waterfront, offering locally grown produce, handmade crafts, and artisan foods.

Market coordinator Tom Eldridge said approximately two hundred visitors attended the opening day, slightly above last year's first-weekend figure. Several new vendors joined this season, including two organic dairy farms and a specialty honey producer.

Operating hours are Saturdays from eight in the morning until one in the afternoon, through the end of September.
